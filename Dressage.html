Dressage

Dressage is often described as a form of dancing on horseback. It emphasizes grace, harmony, and precision, requiring the horse to perform a series of complex movements with seemingly effortless control and responsiveness. Dressage showcases the deep connection and understanding between horse and rider, demonstrating the horse's training and flexibility.

Type of dressage exercises

  1. Piaffe
  2. Passage
  3. Pirouettes
  4. Renvers
