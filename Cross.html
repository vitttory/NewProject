Cross

Cross-country in equestrian sports tests the endurance and speed of the horse and the rider's ability to navigate a variety of obstacles over natural terrain. It's a thrilling discipline that showcases the bravery and trust between horse and rider as they tackle water jumps, ditches, and banks at speed.

Type of cross obstacle

Lake
a small lake through which a horse must run after an obstacle
Banquet
an obstacle where the jump goes from top to bottom
Ditch
jump over a small ditch with water
