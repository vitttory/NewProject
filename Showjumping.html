Showjumping

Showjumping challenges both horse and rider to clear a course of jumps within a set time. This discipline demands agility, accuracy, and the ability to tackle obstacles of varying heights and distances. It's a test of the horse's jumping ability and the rider's strategy and skill in navigating the course efficiently.
